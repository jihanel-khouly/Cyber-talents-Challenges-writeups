## 🏢 Business Gathering: RSA Conference
**[RSA Conference Home](https://www.rsaconference.com/)**

One of the largest and most influential cybersecurity conferences in the world, focusing on cybersecurity, information security, risk management, and privacy. It is attended by global leaders from both technical and executive security roles.

| Feature | Details |
| :--- | :--- |

* **Keynote Speeches:** Insights from global cybersecurity leaders.
* **Technical Sessions:** Ranging from beginner to advanced levels.
* **Hands-on Workshops:** Interactive labs and skill-building.

* Valuable for certifications like **CISSP, CEH, Security+, and CCNA/CCNP Security**.

## 🏴‍☠️ Hackers Gathering: DEF CON
**[The Dark Tangent - defcon.org](https://defcon.org/)**

### What is DEF CON?
The world’s largest and most famous hacker conference. It has a strong focus on offensive security, hacking, and hands-on learning, rooted in underground hacker culture.

| Feature | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Location** | Las Vegas, Nevada, USA |
| **Time** | Annually in August (usually after Black Hat USA) |
| **Registration** | **Cash-only** (emphasizes privacy and anonymity) |

### DEF CON Villages
Specialized areas dedicated to specific hacking topics:
* **Offensive:** Web Hacking, Red Team, IoT, Wireless Attacks.
* **Defensive:** Blue Team, Cloud, AI.
* **Physical:** Lockpicking, Social Engineering.

### Capture The Flag (CTF)
Hosts some of the hardest and most prestigious CTF competitions worldwide, often referred to as the **"Hacker Olympics."**

### Culture & Reputation
* Minimal corporate branding; strong hacker ethics.
* Focuses on zero-day research and live hacking demonstrations.
* Ideal for those seeking real-world, deep technical hacking skills.

## 📜 History: The Creeper Virus (1971)
The **Creeper virus** is widely regarded as the world’s first self-replicating computer program.

* **Creator:** Bob Thomas (Raytheon BBN Technologies).
* **Target:** DEC PDP-10 mainframes running TENEX via ARPANET.
* **Message:** *"I’m the creeper, catch me if you can!"*
* **The First Antivirus:** To counter Creeper, Ray Tomlinson created **Reaper**, a program designed to find and delete Creeper. This marked the beginning of the cybersecurity threat-defense cycle.

## 🏺 Concepts: Steganography & Botnets

### Steganography
* **Origin:** Greek for "covered writing."
* **Definition:** Hiding messages inside other media (images, audio, video).
* **Difference from Cryptography:** Cryptography makes a message unreadable; Steganography hides the fact that a message even exists.

### Botnets
* **Definition:** A network of compromised machines (zombies) controlled by an attacker (botmaster).
* **Mechanism:** Uses Command-and-Control (C2) infrastructure to issue instructions at scale.
* **Risks:** Large-scale DDoS attacks, data theft, and coordinated malicious activities.
